## 1. Purpose

This procedure provides step-by-step guidance for conducting due diligence assessments of third-party technology vendors, implementing POL-VMP-001 and ensuring compliance with SC-GL/2-2023 (R1-2024) outsourcing and third-party risk management requirements.

## 2. Scope

This procedure applies to:

- All new third-party technology vendor engagements
- Renewals or material scope changes to existing vendor contracts
- Market data providers, trading system vendors, cloud service providers, and managed service providers
- Intra-group technology service arrangements treated as third-party engagements

## 4. Scoring Criteria

### 4.1 Assessment Domains

| Domain | Weight | Scoring (1-5) |
|---|---|---|
| **Information security posture** | [25]% | 5=Mature, 4=Established, 3=Defined, 2=Developing, 1=Initial |
| **Financial stability** | [15]% | 5=Strong, 4=Stable, 3=Adequate, 2=Concern, 1=Distressed |
| **Regulatory compliance** | [20]% | 5=Fully compliant, 4=Substantially, 3=Partially, 2=Gaps, 1=Non-compliant |
| **Service capability** | [15]% | 5=Exceeds requirements, 4=Meets, 3=Adequate, 2=Gaps, 1=Insufficient |
| **Business continuity** | [15]% | 5=Tested and mature, 4=Tested, 3=Documented, 2=Partial, 1=None |
| **Concentration risk** | [10]% | 5=No concern, 4=Low, 3=Moderate, 2=High, 1=Critical |

### 4.2 Decision Thresholds

| Weighted Score | Decision |
|---|---|
| 4.0 - 5.0 | Approve — proceed to contracting |
| 3.0 - 3.9 | Conditional approve — remediation plan required before engagement |
| 2.0 - 2.9 | Defer — significant concerns to be addressed |
| Below 2.0 | Reject — unacceptable risk |
