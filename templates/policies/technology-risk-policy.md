## 5. Roles and Responsibilities

### 5.1 Board of Directors

- Approve technology risk appetite and this policy
- Oversee technology risk posture through [quarterly] Board risk reports
- Ensure adequate resources are allocated to technology risk management

### 5.2 Chief Risk Officer (CRO)

- Own and maintain this policy
- Report technology risk exposure to the Board and Risk Committee
- Escalate breaches of risk appetite thresholds

### 5.3 Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)

- Lead cybersecurity risk identification, assessment, and mitigation
- Direct security operations centre (SOC) and incident response functions
- Report security posture metrics to CRO and [Risk Committee]

### 5.4 Chief Technology Officer (CTO)

- Ensure technology architecture supports risk management objectives
- Maintain technology asset inventory and system classifications
- Oversee system development lifecycle (SDLC) controls

### 5.5 Three Lines of Defence

| Line | Function | Responsibility |
|---|---|---|
| **1st Line** | Business units and IT operations | Own and manage technology risks day-to-day |
| **2nd Line** | Risk management and compliance | Provide oversight, frameworks, and challenge |
| **3rd Line** | Internal audit | Independent assurance on control effectiveness |
