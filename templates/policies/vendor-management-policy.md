## 3. Vendor Classification

All vendors shall be classified based on criticality to capital market operations:

| Tier | Criteria | Examples | Assessment Frequency |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Critical** | Disruption would impact trading, settlement, or regulatory compliance | Trading platform providers, market data vendors, core banking/custody systems | [Semi-annually] |
| **High** | Disruption would materially impact business operations | Cloud infrastructure, network providers, cybersecurity tools | [Annually] |
| **Medium** | Disruption would cause operational inconvenience | HR systems, office productivity, non-critical SaaS | [Every 2 years] |
| **Low** | Minimal operational impact | General suppliers, stationery, non-IT services | At onboarding only |

- Classification shall be reviewed [annually] or upon material change in vendor scope
- The Securities Commission Malaysia shall be notified of outsourcing arrangements as required by SC-GL/6-2023

## 4. Due Diligence

Pre-engagement due diligence shall be conducted per PRO-VAS-001, covering:

- Financial viability and business continuity capability
- Information security posture (certifications, audit reports, penetration test results)
- Regulatory compliance track record
- Data handling practices and data residency
- References from comparable capital market entities
- Concentration risk assessment — dependency across [Organisation Name]'s critical functions
