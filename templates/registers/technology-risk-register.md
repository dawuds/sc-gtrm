## Likelihood Scale

| Score | Level | Description |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Rare | Less than once per 5 years |
| 2 | Unlikely | Once per 2-5 years |
| 3 | Possible | Once per 1-2 years |
| 4 | Likely | Multiple times per year |
| 5 | Almost Certain | Monthly or more frequent |

## Impact Scale

| Score | Level | Financial (RM) | Market Impact | Regulatory |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Insignificant | < 100K | No market effect | No SC interest |
| 2 | Minor | 100K - 1M | Brief disruption, no client impact | SC inquiry |
| 3 | Moderate | 1M - 10M | Trading disruption < 4 hours | SC investigation |
| 4 | Major | 10M - 50M | Trading disruption > 4 hours, client losses | SC enforcement action |
| 5 | Catastrophic | > 50M | Market-wide impact, settlement failure | License suspension/revocation |
